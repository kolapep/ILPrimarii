Expirimental Corpse

10/4/2004

I played the game with a few of my friends and asked them to design simple monsters with backstories for me. I resketched their drawings onto a readable page and then constructed my final monster with the outline sheet given. I decided to go simple with the colors (black and white) and focused on using stokeweight to replicate lineweight of inking in tradtiional media.
